Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 medium brown or white onion, peeled and diced
  • 2 clovs garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1 tsp chili powder (mild)
  • 1 tbsp boullion
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 choice of meat
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper (or more to taste-- spicy!)
  • Pinch of sugar (optional, to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tbsp fresh chopped green onions
  • 2 cups cold cooked rice

Fried Rice

Description

Jared's Fried Rice is an easy to make, inexpensive, fried rice with loads of flavor.

Directions

  1. Cook rice, preferably in a rice cooker, let sit in refrigerator for 30 minutes. If cooking with meat, prepare meat.
  2. While rice is cooking, add sesame oil, garlic, and onion to pan. Cook until fragrant.
  3. Crack eggs into bowl, whisk.
  4. Add rice to the pan, once the garlic and onions have been nicely cooked.
  5. Pour whisked eggs over the rice once warm enough.
  6. Add boullion, preferably with some water to mix into the pan. Let simmer and stir for 2 minutes.
  7. Add soy sauce, peas, green onions, and other spices to the pan. Mix well until rice is an even brown color.
  8. Garnish with green onions! Let sit out to cool, and enjoy.
